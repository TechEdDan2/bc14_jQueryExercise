Add Titles & Ratings

This is a solution to a Movie App Challenge which includes:

  • Creating a form that requies two inputs for a movie: name and rating
  • When the form is submitted, capture the values for each of the inputs and append them to the DOM along with a button to remove each title and rating from the DOM.
  • When the button to remove is clicked, remove each title and rating from the DOM.
  • A way to ensure that the rating of a movie can only be between 0 and 10.
  • A way to ensure that a title has at least 2 characters in it.
  • Allow users to sort alphabetically by the title of the movie or by the rating of the movie from lowest to highest and vice versa.
